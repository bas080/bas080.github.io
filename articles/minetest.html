Minetest

You might have noticed that I have quite some Minetest mods hosted on Github. They allow people to add features like tiny trees, a mailbox, vines and different textures to their game. But why would I spend hours of my time making these mods? Sometimes it's nice to work on code for no particular reason.

Play is something people do all the time. I happen to code and share that work with people. Why do I play?. I'm not able to answer that question. It just feels like something I should be doing. For me it's the most constructive way to distract myself.

Read more about play at Wikipedia. They will most likely do a better job at describing the concepts of play.
